## 🗺️ The 6 pages (top menu / the ☰ menu on phone)

### 1. **Home**
Just scroll slowly. It's your whole story — your life, in chapters.
- **Tap any photo** to see it big. **Tap any video** (the ▶ ones) to play it.
- Watch the gold **heartbeat line** — yes, the website has a pulse. You're a cardiologist. 🫀
- The numbers count up: 30+ years, 500+ loved ones, 6+ countries…
- At the very bottom is **a letter from us**. Read that part slowly.

### 2. **His Story**
The chapters again, top to bottom — the healer, Tanzania, the family, the way you love.
- In **"The Little Things,"** tap **💬 Give me a Munaf‑ism** — the AI gives you a piece of *your own* wisdom. (Tap again for a new one.)

### 3. **Family** 🌳
Your whole family tree.
- Tap a **name** to open or close that branch.
- Switch branches at the top: **Shamji side · Mom's (Sunderji) · Mom's (Esmail)**.
- See someone missing? Tap **"+ add a relative"** and add them yourself. (It saves on your device.)

### 4. **Gallery** 📸
Every photo and video in one place. **Tap any of them** to view full‑screen. Swipe / use ‹ › to move through them.

### 5. **Ask Munaf** 🤖 — *the fun part*
This is an **AI of you**, in your voice. Try all of it:
- **😄 Tell me a fresh Munaf joke** — a brand‑new joke, every tap.
- **In his own words** — your *actual* real jokes (the waiting‑room one!).
- **The friend bit:** type a name, tap **"Oh, they talk about you all the time!"** — watch it do your classic move. 😂
- **Ask him anything:** type a question (or tap a suggestion) and chat. Ask it for advice. Ask what to do when you're tired. It answers the way you would.

### 6. **Dad's Desk** ✍️ — *built for you to actually use*
- **To‑do list:** write your to‑dos (one per line) → tap **Send by Message** → it opens your texts, already addressed to **Zain, Reza & Mom**. Hit send. (No more paper!)
- **Share an article:** paste a link *or* snap a photo of the newspaper — it reads the headline — then send it to us with one tap.
- **Your songs:** all your favorites, with links to play them.
